# Rooted Intelligence - Sustainable AI & Creative Harmony

"Technology Grows From Ecological Wealth, Not At Its Expense"

## Project Overview
Rooted Intelligence is a front-end development group project dedicated to creating a concept for a flagship AI startup. The site explores AI solutions that harmonize with the natural world, focusing on ethical practices and sustainable development.

## Key Features
- **Sustainable AI Hub**: A specialized portal showcasing the mission, ethics, and business impact of Rooted Intelligence.
- **Rhythmic Integration**: A unique look at how technology can draw inspiration from natural rhythms and creative arts like drumming.
- **Modern Tech Aesthetic**: A premium UI with neon-inspired branding, glassmorphism, and responsive layouts.
- **Thematic Continuity**: Multiple curated pages (Mission, Ethics, Case Studies) that maintain a consistent, high-impact visual identity.

## Technologies Used
- HTML5 (Semantic Structure)
- CSS3 (Custom Styling, Animations)
- JavaScript (Scroll Observers, Dynamic UI)
- AI-Generated Brand Assets
