Navy Discipline & The Mission

As a veteran of the United States Navy, I carry the values of discipline, focus, and teamwork into everything I do. Serving in the Navy taught me that while individual excellence is important, the bigger task always requires a unified crew.

Whether I'm mastering a complex poly-rhythm or coordinating a collaborative effort like this dashboard, the goal is always clear: Mission Success.

Rooted Intelligence is more than a technical project; it's an exercise in collaborative teamwork.
